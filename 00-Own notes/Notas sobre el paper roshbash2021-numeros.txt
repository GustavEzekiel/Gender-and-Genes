scRNA-Seq data aligned to the Drosophila genome (dm6) using zUMIs (Parekh et al., 2018), together with STAR with default setting (Dobin et al., 2013). 
Only the alignments to annotated exons were used for UMI quantitation. Next, we filtered out low-quality cells using the following criteria: 
(1) fewer than 1000 or more than 6000 detected genes (where each gene had to have at least one UMI aligned); (2) fewer than 6000 or more than 75000 
total UMI; (3) gene expression entropy smaller than 5.5, where entropy was defined as -nUMI * ln(nUMI) for genes with nUMI >0, where nUMI was a number 
of UMI in a cell.
