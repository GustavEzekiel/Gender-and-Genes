NOTA QUE APARECIÓ EN LAS FIGURAS SUPLEMETARIAS:

S5 Fig. ChAT expression in the LNds is important for sleep suppression.
Transcripts encoding choline acetyltransferase (ChAT) are enriched in LNds. Knockdown of ChAT transcripts specifically in the LNds 
(Dv-PDF-GAL4, pdfgal80; pdfgal80 driver) using RNAi results in an increase in total sleep (black). This increase is statistically 
significant (p-value <0.05 by one-way Anova) compared to both the driver only control (white) and the RNAi only control (gray). n = 16 
for all genotypes.
